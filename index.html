Professional Tree Services in Surrey

Cabot's Trees & Hedges are based in Woking, set up by Alastair Cabot. Ali has many years of experience as an Arborist, cutting trees since he left school, quickly moving up the ranks to manage a team of people on site. He is a fully qualified expert tree surgeon with qualifications in tree maintenance, health and safety, and a felling license from the Arboricultural Association.

Cabot's Trees operate throughout Surrey, Berkshire, and Hampshire, offering a full range of tree services for commercial and residential sectors. Professional expertise combined with a high level of politeness make up our highly skilled team.
